# Regex of Countries  

References:  
1. [ISO-3166](https://www.iso.org/iso-3166-country-codes.html)  
2. [ISO-3166-1](https://www.iso.org/standard/72482.html)

## Asia  

China:  
`(?i)(.*(🇨🇳|c(hi?)?na?|中国).*)`  

Hong Kong:  
`(?i)(.*(🇭🇰|h(ong\s?)?k((on)?g)?|香?港).*)`   

India:  
`(?i)(.*(🇮🇳|in(d(ia)?)?|印度).*)`  

Indonesia:  
`(?i)(.*(🇮🇩|in?d(o?n(esia)?)?|印度尼西亚).*)`  

Japan:  
`(?i)(.*(🇯🇵|ja?p(a?n)?|日本?).*)`  

Korea:  
`(?i)(.*(🇰🇷|ko?r(ea)?|韩国?).*)`  

Macao:  
`(?i)(.*(🇲🇴|m(aca?)?o?|澳门?).*)`  

Malaysia:  
`(?i)(.*(🇲🇾|m(ala)?y(s(ia)?)?|马来西亚).*)`  

Philippines:  
`(?i)(.*(🇵🇭|ph(i?l(ippines)?)?|菲(律宾)?).*)`  

Singapore:  
`(?i)(.*(🇸🇬|s(in)?g(a?p(ore)?)?|新加坡).*)`  

Taiwan:  
`(?i)(.*(🇹🇼|t(ai)?w(a?n)?|台湾?).*)`  

Thailand:  
`(?i)(.*(🇹🇭|th(a(iland)?)?|泰国).*)`  

Viet Nam:  
`(?i)(.*(🇻🇳|v(iet\s?)?n(a?m)?|越南).*)`  

## North America  

Mexico:  
`(?i)(.*(🇲🇽|me?x(ico)?|墨西哥).*)`  

Canada:  
`(?i)(.*(🇨🇦|ca(n(ada)?)?|加(拿大)?).*)`  

United States of America:  
`(?i)(.*(🇺🇸|u(nited\s?)?s(tates)?(\so?f\s?)?(a(merica)?)?|美((利坚合众)?国)?).*)`  

## South America  

Argentina:  
`(?i)(.*(🇦🇷|(ar(g(entina)?)?|阿根廷).*)`  

Brazil:  
`(?i)(.*(🇧🇷|br(a(zil)?)?|巴西).*)`  

Colombia:  
`(?i)(.*(🇨🇴|co(l(ombia)?)?|哥伦比亚).*)`  

## Europe

`(?i)(.*(🇦🇿|az(e(rbaijan)?)?|阿塞拜疆).*)`

`(?i)(.*(🇫🇷|fr(a(nce)?)?|法国).*)`  

Germany:  
`(?i)(.*(🇩🇪|(germany)?(DEU?)?|德国).*)`

`(?i)(.*(🇮🇸|i(ce)?s?(l(and)?)?|冰岛).*)`

`(?i)(.*(🇮🇪|(ir?e(land)?|爱尔兰).*)`
